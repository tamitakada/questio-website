WHAT IS QUESTIO?

Questio is a flashcard app that lets you study through timed quizzes. You'll earn points, collect achievements, and unlock special badges just by studying. Gone are the days of slogging through piles of paper index cards. With Questio's simple flashcard creator and multiple quiz modes, studying flashcards is hassle-free and fun!

>>

STEPS TO QUESTIO

1. CREATE A FLASHCARD SET

Create flashcards on the fly with the set editor, and organize sets with six different color categories.

2. STUDY FLASHCARDS WITH QUIZ MODE

Study your flashcards through two different timed quiz modes. Questio also lets you customize quiz settings to match your unique needs.

3. EARN POINTS & COLLECT BAGDES

Earn points just by studying your flashcards through quizzes. When you earn enough points and unlock specific achievements, you can earn badges to display on your home page.
